About Lado Foundation

Every day, thousands of children in India go to bed hungry and miss out on learning. Lado Foundation is committed to changing this. We provide meals, school supplies, and access to education for underprivileged children. Your support can give a child the chance they deserve — to learn, to grow, and to dream. Every donation, no matter how small, can change a life forever.

What we do:

  • Provide nutritious meals so children can focus on learning.
  • Supply books, uniforms and school materials.
  • Support local volunteer-run classes and mentorship.

Sachin — Founder

I started Lado Foundation because I believe every child deserves the right to education and a full stomach. With your help, we can provide consistent meals and learning materials to children who need them most.
